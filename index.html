Monastery fields
Who are we?

"Monastery fields" operates within the revered grounds of the monastery "St. Bogoroditsa" in the picturesque village of Kurtovo Konare, nestled in the heart of the Plovdiv region. Our organization stands as a registered and reputable agricultural producer, committed to delivering excellence in our cultivation practices.

Our company upholds the highest standards of agricultural integrity, nurturing our crops from certified seeds with meticulous attention to detail. Our cultivation methods eschew the use of pesticides and artificial fertilizers, ensuring the preservation of natural ecosystems and the promotion of ecological balance.

Central to our ethos is the cultivation of premium-quality potatoes renowned for their exceptional taste profile. Characterized by a dense texture and distinctly sweet flavor, our potatoes embody culinary excellence. When prepared, they boast a tantarizing crispness on the exterior, coupled with a sumptuously soft interior. Furthermore, our potatoes demonstrate remarkable resilience in the kitchen, maintaining their shape and texture throughout the cooking process.
